Incorporation Services

One of our key areas of expertise lies in providing the end-to-end services required to help independent entrepreneurs and businesses establish new ventures. From structuring the entity to registration and licensing, we ensure a smooth journey for starting your business.

Business Legal Structure

We recommend the most appropriate legal structure for your business after analyzing ownership, liability, taxation, and scalability factors.

Documentation

Expert assistance in drafting and finalizing incorporation documents like Memorandum of Association (MoA), Articles of Association (AoA), partnership deeds, trust deeds, and related filings.

Entity Registration

Complete support in registering your entity with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Registrar of Firms, or other statutory bodies.

Types of Enterprises We Help Incorporate

Private Limited Company

Formation of Private Companies under The Companies Act, ideal for startups and small businesses requiring limited liability and growth potential.

Public Limited Company

Incorporation of Public Companies under The Companies Act, suited for larger businesses looking to raise capital from the public.

Charitable Societies & Section 8 Companies

Assistance in forming Charitable Societies under the Charitable Societies Act and Section 8 Companies for non-profit initiatives.

Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)

Registration of LLPs that combine the flexibility of a partnership with the benefits of limited liability for its partners.

Public & Private Trusts

Incorporation of Public and Private Trusts under The Trust Act for charitable, educational, and philanthropic purposes.

Partnership Firms

Setup of Partnership Firms under The Partnership Act, including drafting partnership deeds and registering with local authorities.

Licensing & Registrations

JDNT also provides end-to-end assistance and guidance in obtaining necessary registrations and licenses for commencement of business, including GST, PAN, TAN, Import-Export Code (IEC), and other industry-specific approvals.
