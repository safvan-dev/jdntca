Consultancy Services

In today’s competitive environment, every organization aspires to achieve sound and functional management of their operations. At JDNT & Associates, we offer a comprehensive suite of consultancy programs designed to deliver the best possible solutions for smooth functioning, efficiency, and growth of institutions.

Financial Management

Expert support in financial management planning and financial policy determination to ensure long-term stability and growth.

Working Capital Management

Helping businesses optimize working capital to maintain liquidity, improve profitability, and achieve operational efficiency.

Project Reports & Feasibility Studies

Preparation of detailed project reports and feasibility studies to support new ventures, expansions, and investment decisions.

Cash & Profitability Analysis

Preparation of cash budgets, cash flow statements, profitability statements, and fund flow reports to strengthen financial decision-making.

Budgeting

Designing and monitoring capital and revenue budgets to control costs and enhance financial discipline across operations.

Management Accounting & Cost Control

Implementation of management accounting systems, cost control measures, and value analysis to improve efficiency and profitability.

MIS & Reporting Systems

Business Policy & Corporate Planning

Advisory on business policy, corporate planning, organizational development, growth, and diversification strategies to ensure long-term competitiveness.
