Taxation Services

One of the major strengths of JDNT & Associates is our unmatched expertise in handling taxation matters for a wide range of entities. We provide comprehensive Direct and Indirect Tax Services to ensure compliance, minimize tax liability, and guide businesses through complex tax landscapes.

Tax Audit

Our team conducts Tax Audits under both the Income Tax Act and GST Act, ensuring accuracy, compliance, and timely reporting.

  • Verification of books of accounts and records.
  • Preparation of audit reports in Form 3CA/3CB & 3CD.
  • Ensuring compliance with GST provisions.

Tax Representations

We represent clients before tax authorities at various levels, providing expert assistance in resolving disputes and assessments.

  • Handling notices, assessments, and appeals.
  • Liaison with Income Tax, GST, and other authorities.
  • End-to-end support until case resolution.

Direct & Indirect Tax Advisory

Our advisory services guide clients through complex tax structures, ensuring compliance and minimizing risk.

  • Expert opinion on corporate & personal taxation.
  • Structuring of business transactions for tax efficiency.
  • Support in navigating new tax laws & amendments.

Tax Planning

We design effective tax planning strategies to minimize tax incidence, strictly within the framework of applicable law.

  • Optimization of tax liability.
  • Utilization of available deductions & exemptions.
  • Strategic long-term planning for businesses & individuals.

TDS & Double Taxation Compliance

We ensure smooth compliance with TDS requirements and international Double Taxation Avoidance Treaties (DTAA).

  • TDS deduction & return filing compliance.
  • Advisory on DTAA applicability.
  • Prevention of double taxation in cross-border operations.

Opinions on Complex Tax Issues

We provide expert legal and technical opinions on intricate taxation matters to help businesses take informed decisions.

  • Clarification on evolving tax laws.
  • Interpretation of ambiguous provisions.
  • Support for litigation and compliance strategies.

Recurring Tax Compliances

We manage all recurring tax obligations, ensuring timely compliance and peace of mind.

  • Filing of income tax, GST, and other tax returns.
  • Maintenance of tax records and registers.
  • Periodic reviews to prevent penalties & interest liabilities.
