Statutory Audit

Ensuring compliance, accuracy, and transparency through statutory financial audits.

Comprehensive Statutory Audit Services

At JDNT & Associates, we provide statutory audit solutions tailored to meet regulatory requirements while adding value to your business. Our audits ensure compliance, transparency, accuracy, and accountability.

Audit of Companies

We conduct statutory audits of public and private limited companies under the Companies Act, 2013, ensuring accurate representation of financial performance.

Key Focus Areas:
  • Verification of financial statements and compliance with Accounting Standards.
  • Evaluation of internal controls and risk management systems.
  • Ensuring corporate governance and disclosure requirements.

Firms & Proprietorship

We audit partnership firms and proprietary concerns, supporting growth and compliance across industries.

Highlights:
  • Business performance analysis with accurate reporting.
  • Regulatory compliance verification for smooth operations.
  • Strengthening internal systems and accountability.

Banks & Insurance Companies

Specialized audits designed for financial institutions with a focus on risk management and compliance.

Key Features:
  • Compliance with RBI/IRDA guidelines and statutory laws.
  • Risk-based audit methodology for detecting irregularities.
  • Focus on asset quality, provisioning, and fraud prevention.

Non-Profit Organizations

We bring transparency to charitable trusts and NGOs, ensuring accountability in the use of funds.

Audit Coverage:
  • Fund utilization and donor reporting audits.
  • Grant compliance checks.
  • Verification of statutory filings and disclosures.

Tax Audit

Conducted under Section 44AB of the Income Tax Act, 1961, our tax audits ensure compliance and reduce future tax risks.

Includes:
  • Income tax compliance and filing accuracy.
  • Assessment of tax planning and optimization.
  • Preparation of Form 3CA/3CB and 3CD.

GST & Sales Tax Audit

Detailed audits under the GST Act and Sales Tax Acts to ensure accurate compliance and reporting.

Core Areas:
  • Reconciliation of GST returns with financial statements.
  • Verification of input tax credit claims.
  • Ensuring compliance with GST laws and circulars.
