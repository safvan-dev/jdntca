About JDNT & Associates

Chartered Accountants since 1984 — trusted by businesses across industries for audit, taxation, advisory, and consulting solutions.

Our Journey Since 1984

JDNT & Associates, the firm of Chartered Accountants, established in the year 1984, serves diverse industries by providing quality services to clients. Our client profile includes both medium-sized businesses and multinational companies across industries such as banking, manufacturing, IT & ITES, trading, real estate, hospitality, and consultancy.

Our partners and talented staff members have wide-ranging experience in audit, taxation, management consulting, and accounting services. With more than three decades of partner-level expertise, we bring practical and result-oriented approaches, ensuring superior client satisfaction.
